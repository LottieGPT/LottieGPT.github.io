LottieGPT: Tokenizing Vector Animation for Autoregressive Generation

CVPR 2026 Kejun Gao*, [Co-authors...]

Abstract: We present LottieGPT, a novel framework that tokenizes Lottie vector animations into a discrete vocabulary, enabling Large Language Models to generate high-quality, structured vector animations.
